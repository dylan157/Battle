WELCOME TO BATTLE!

How to play:

Choose a name,

Walk to the spawn pad (~~ bottom right corner)

To walk simply enter w, a, s, d followed by enter.

After stepping on the spawn pad you will earn 25 gold.

Go to the shop and buy access to area 0 for 10 gold.

Now exit the store by entering 'e'

Find an enemy in zone 0 and kill him!

After all the enemy are dead, walk to the spawn icon again. 

Keep battling untill you meet BOSSANI
